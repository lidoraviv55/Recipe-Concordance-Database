=== HEADNOTE ===
Thin crepes filled with sweetened cheese and pan-fried until golden. A traditional dairy dish often served on holidays.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 3 eggs
2. 1 cup milk
3. 1 cup all-purpose flour
4. 1/4 tsp salt
5. 2 tbsp butter, melted (plus more for frying)
6. 500 g farmer cheese or ricotta
7. 1 egg yolk
8. 3 tbsp sugar
9. 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
10. zest of 1 lemon
11. sour cream, for serving
12. fresh berries or jam, for serving

=== STEPS ===
1. Blend eggs, milk, flour, salt, and melted butter until smooth; let rest 30 minutes.
2. Heat 18 cm (7 inch) non-stick pan over medium heat; brush lightly with butter; pour 3 tbsp batter, swirl to coat bottom; cook until edges lift and bottom is set, about 1 minute; flip onto clean towel, cooked side up; repeat with remaining batter.
3. Mix farmer cheese, egg yolk, sugar, vanilla, and lemon zest until smooth.
4. Place 2 tbsp filling on lower third of each crepe (cooked side up); fold bottom over filling, fold in sides, and roll up.
5. Fry blintzes seam-side down in butter over medium heat until golden, about 2 minutes per side; serve warm with sour cream and berries.
