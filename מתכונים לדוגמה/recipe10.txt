=== HEADNOTE ===
Creamy baked noodle pudding with cheese and a lightly sweet finish.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 250 g wide egg noodles
2. 4 tbsp butter, melted (plus for greasing)
3. 500 g cottage cheese
4. 1 cup sour cream
5. 3 eggs
6. 1/2 cup sugar
7. 1 tsp vanilla extract
8. 1/2 tsp salt
9. 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
10. ground cinnamon, for top (optional)

=== STEPS ===
1. Heat oven to 180°C/355°F. Grease a 9×13 inch (23×33 cm) pan.
2. Boil noodles in salted water until barely tender; drain and toss with butter.
3. Whisk cottage cheese, sour cream, eggs, sugar, vanilla, and salt. Fold in noodles (and raisins, if using).
4. Spread in pan; dust with cinnamon if desired. Bake 35–45 minutes until set and golden at edges. Rest 10 minutes before serving.
