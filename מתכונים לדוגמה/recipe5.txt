=== RECIPE ===
TITLE: GEFILLTE FISCH
SECTION: FISH

VARIANT_NO:
KASHRUT_TYPE: PARVE
HOLIDAY_FLAGS:
PRIMARY_INGREDIENT: Pike/Carp
METHODS: BOILED
YIELD_TEXT: 12 patties
SERVINGS: 6

=== HEADNOTE ===
Poached fish quenelles in a lightly sweet stock with carrot rounds.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 2 lb (900 g) pike or carp fillets, skin removed
2. 2 medium onions, 1 grated and 1 sliced
3. 2 eggs
4. 1/2 cup matzo meal
5. 1 1/2 tsp salt (divided)
6. 1 tsp sugar (plus more to taste for stock)
7. 1/4 tsp black pepper
8. 2 carrots, sliced
9. 6 cups water

=== STEPS ===
1. Grind fish with grated onion. Mix in eggs, matzo meal, 1 tsp salt, sugar, and pepper until cohesive.
2. Form oval patties with wet hands.
3. In a wide pot, place sliced onion, carrots, water, remaining 1/2 tsp salt, and a pinch of sugar; bring to a gentle boil.
4. Lower patties into the simmering stock; cover and poach 45–60 minutes.
5. Chill in the stock; serve cold with carrot slices.
