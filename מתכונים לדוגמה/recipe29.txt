=== HEADNOTE ===
Golden roasted chicken with crispy skin and juicy meat. The quintessential Friday night dinner centerpiece.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 whole chicken (about 1.5 kg)
2. 3 tbsp olive oil
3. 1 tsp salt
4. 1/2 tsp black pepper
5. 1 tsp paprika
6. 4 cloves garlic, minced
7. 1 lemon, quartered
8. 4 sprigs fresh thyme
9. 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
10. 1 onion, quartered

=== STEPS ===
1. Remove chicken from refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking; pat dry with paper towels.
2. Mix olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic; rub all over chicken and under skin.
3. Stuff cavity with lemon, thyme, rosemary, and onion.
4. Place breast-side up in roasting pan; roast at 200°C/400°F for 1 hour 15 minutes until skin is golden and juices run clear.
5. Let rest 10 minutes before carving; serve with pan juices.
