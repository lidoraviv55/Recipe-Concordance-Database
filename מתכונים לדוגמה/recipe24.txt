YIELD_TEXT: 12 rolls
SERVINGS: 6

=== HEADNOTE ===
Tender cabbage leaves wrapped around seasoned meat and rice, simmered in sweet and sour tomato sauce.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 large head cabbage
2. 500 g ground beef
3. 1/2 cup uncooked rice
4. 1 onion, grated
5. 1 egg
6. 1 tsp salt
7. 1/4 tsp pepper
8. 2 cups tomato sauce
9. 1/4 cup brown sugar
10. 3 tbsp lemon juice
11. 1/2 cup raisins

=== STEPS ===
1. Core cabbage and place in large pot of boiling water; cook 5 minutes until outer leaves soften; carefully remove 12 large leaves; shave thick ribs flat.
2. Mix beef, rice, onion, egg, salt, and pepper until well combined.
3. Place 2–3 tbsp filling on each cabbage leaf; fold sides in and roll tightly; place seam-side down in Dutch oven.
4. Combine tomato sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice, and raisins; pour over cabbage rolls.
5. Cover and simmer over low heat for 2 hours, or bake at 160°C/325°F for 2 hours; baste occasionally with sauce.
