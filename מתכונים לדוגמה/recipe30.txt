=== HEADNOTE ===
Dense and creamy cheesecake with graham cracker crust. A Jewish deli classic that became an American icon.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
2. 1/4 cup sugar
3. 6 tbsp butter, melted
4. 1 kg cream cheese, softened
5. 1 cup sugar
6. 5 eggs
7. 2 tsp vanilla extract
8. 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
9. 1 cup sour cream

=== STEPS ===
1. Mix graham crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter; press firmly into bottom and 2 cm up sides of 23 cm (9-inch) springform pan; refrigerate while making filling.
2. Beat cream cheese until smooth; add 1 cup sugar gradually; beat until fluffy.
3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each; mix in vanilla, flour, and sour cream until just combined.
4. Pour into crust; bake at 160°C/325°F for 1 hour 15 minutes until set but slightly jiggly in center; turn off oven and leave door cracked for 1 hour.
5. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight before serving; run knife around edge before releasing springform.
