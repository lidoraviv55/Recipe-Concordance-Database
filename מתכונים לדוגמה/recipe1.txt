=== HEADNOTE ===
A classic canapé spread of sardines brightened with lemon and parsley, served on crisp toast.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 6 sardines (canned in oil), skinned and boned
2. 2 tbsp lemon juice
3. 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
4. 2 tbsp chopped parsley
5. 4 tbsp olive oil
6. 12 thin toast rounds (about 1/2 inch thick)
7. 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped (optional)
8. salt, to taste
9. black pepper, to taste

=== STEPS ===
1. In a bowl, mash sardines to a paste. Stir in lemon juice, Worcestershire, parsley, and 2 tbsp olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Brush bread lightly with remaining olive oil and bake at 200°C/390°F until just golden (about 5 minutes), or toast.
3. Spread sardine paste over each toast. If using, top with chopped egg and a pinch of parsley. Serve immediately.
