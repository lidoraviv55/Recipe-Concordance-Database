=== HEADNOTE ===
Traditional braided egg bread for Shabbat and holidays. The eggs give it a rich golden color and tender crumb.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 cup warm water (about 40°C/105°F)
2. 2 tsp active dry yeast
3. 1/2 cup sugar
4. 1/3 cup vegetable oil
5. 2 eggs, beaten
6. 1 tsp salt
7. 4 cups all-purpose flour (plus more for kneading)
8. 1 egg yolk, for glaze
9. 1 tbsp water, for glaze
10. sesame or poppy seeds (optional)

=== STEPS ===
1. Dissolve yeast in warm water with a pinch of sugar; let stand 10 minutes until foamy.
2. Stir in remaining sugar, oil, eggs, and salt; add flour gradually, mixing until a soft dough forms.
3. Knead on floured surface 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic; place in oiled bowl, cover, and let rise 1½ hours until doubled.
4. Punch down dough; divide in half, then divide each half into 3 pieces; roll into ropes, braid, place on greased baking sheets, cover, and let rise 45 minutes.
5. Heat oven to 180°C/350°F; beat egg yolk with water, brush over loaves, sprinkle with seeds if desired, and bake 30–35 minutes until golden.
