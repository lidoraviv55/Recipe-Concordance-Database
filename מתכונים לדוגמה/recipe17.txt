=== HEADNOTE ===
Traditional poached fish dumplings served cold with horseradish. A labor of love, best made a day ahead.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 kg mixed white fish fillets (carp, pike, whitefish)
2. 2 medium onions, quartered
3. 2 eggs
4. 2 tbsp matzo meal
5. 1 tbsp sugar
6. 2 tsp salt
7. 1/2 tsp white pepper
8. 1/4 cup ice water
9. fish bones and heads (for stock)
10. 2 carrots, sliced
11. 2 stalks celery
12. prepared horseradish, for serving

=== STEPS ===
1. Make stock: simmer fish bones, 1 onion, carrots, celery, and 2 liters water for 30 minutes; strain and return to pot.
2. Grind fish and remaining onion in food processor until smooth; add eggs, matzo meal, sugar, salt, pepper, and ice water; process until light.
3. With wet hands, form mixture into oval patties about 8 cm (3 inches) long.
4. Bring stock to gentle simmer; carefully lower fish patties into stock; cover and poach 1½ hours, basting occasionally.
5. Remove fish to serving platter; top each with carrot slice; strain and reduce cooking liquid; pour over fish; refrigerate overnight; serve cold with horseradish.
