=== HEADNOTE ===
Tender chicken pieces gently stewed with onions and light gravy.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 whole chicken (about 1.5 kg), cut into serving pieces
2. 2 tbsp schmaltz (or neutral oil)
3. 2 medium onions, sliced
4. 2 tbsp flour
5. 3 cups water or stock
6. 1 bay leaf
7. 1 tsp salt, or to taste
8. black pepper, to taste
9. 1 tbsp chopped parsley (for serving)

=== STEPS ===
1. Heat schmaltz in a wide pot; brown onions lightly. Add chicken and sauté until just colored.
2. Sprinkle flour; stir 1 minute. Add water/stock and bay leaf; bring to a simmer.
3. Cover and stew gently 35–45 minutes until chicken is tender. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
