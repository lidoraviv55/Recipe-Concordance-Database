=== HEADNOTE ===
Beef and onions richly seasoned with paprika; hearty and aromatic.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 kg beef chuck, cut in 3 cm cubes
2. 3 tbsp oil or schmaltz
3. 3 large onions, sliced
4. 2 tbsp sweet Hungarian paprika
5. 2 cloves garlic, minced
6. 2 tsp salt (to taste)
7. 1 tsp caraway seeds (optional)
8. 2 medium tomatoes, chopped (or 1/2 cup crushed)
9. 2 cups water or stock
10. black pepper, to taste

=== STEPS ===
1. Sauté onions in fat until deep golden.
2. Add beef; brown lightly. Stir in paprika and garlic; cook 30 seconds.
3. Add tomatoes, caraway (if using), salt, pepper, and water/stock.
4. Cover and simmer gently 1.5–2 hours until beef is tender; adjust seasoning.
