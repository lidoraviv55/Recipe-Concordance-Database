METHODS: BOILED
YIELD_TEXT: About 2 liters
SERVINGS: 6

=== HEADNOTE ===
Bright beet soup with a gentle sweet-and-sour balance; serve hot or chilled.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 4 medium beets, peeled and grated (or cut in matchsticks)
2. 1 medium onion, sliced
3. 6 cups water
4. 2 tbsp lemon juice (or to taste)
5. 2 tbsp sugar (or to taste)
6. 1 tsp salt, or to taste
7. black pepper, to taste
8. fresh dill, chopped (for serving, optional)

=== STEPS ===
1. Combine beets, onion, and water in a pot; bring to a simmer and cook until beets are tender (30–40 minutes).
2. Season with lemon juice, sugar, salt, and pepper to a sweet–sour balance.
3. Serve hot or chilled, with chopped dill if desired.
