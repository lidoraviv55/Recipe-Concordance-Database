=== HEADNOTE ===
Flaky cream cheese pastries rolled with cinnamon, nuts, and raisins. The dough must be well chilled for easy handling.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 225 g cream cheese, softened
2. 225 g (1 cup) butter, softened
3. 2 cups all-purpose flour
4. 1/4 tsp salt
5. 1/2 cup apricot jam
6. 1/2 cup brown sugar
7. 1 tsp ground cinnamon
8. 1 cup walnuts, finely chopped
9. 1/2 cup raisins
10. 1 egg, beaten (for glaze)
11. coarse sugar, for sprinkling

=== STEPS ===
1. Beat cream cheese and butter until smooth; mix in flour and salt until dough forms; divide into 4 discs, wrap, and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
2. Mix brown sugar, cinnamon, walnuts, and raisins in small bowl.
3. Roll one disc into 25 cm (10 inch) circle on floured surface; spread thin layer of jam, then sprinkle with quarter of nut mixture.
4. Cut into 8 wedges; roll each from wide end to point; curve into crescent shape; place on parchment-lined baking sheet; repeat with remaining dough.
5. Brush with beaten egg; sprinkle with coarse sugar; bake at 180°C/350°F for 20–25 minutes until golden; cool on wire rack.
