=== HEADNOTE ===
Classic deviled eggs finished with a quick stovetop milk sauce.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 6 eggs
2. 1 tbsp butter
3. 1 tbsp flour
4. 1 cup milk
5. 1 tsp prepared mustard
6. 1 tbsp chopped parsley
7. pinch cayenne
8. salt, to taste
9. black pepper, to taste

=== STEPS ===
1. Hard-boil eggs (10 minutes), chill, peel, and halve lengthwise. Mash yolks with mustard, a pinch of salt and pepper; refill whites.
2. For the sauce: melt butter, whisk in flour; cook 1 minute. Whisk in milk and cook until thick. Season with salt, pepper, cayenne; stir in parsley.
3. Spoon hot sauce over the filled eggs and serve at once.
