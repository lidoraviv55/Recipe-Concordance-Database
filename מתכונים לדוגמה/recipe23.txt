=== HEADNOTE ===
Rich and savory appetizer spread made from chicken livers, hard-boiled eggs, and schmaltz. A traditional Shabbat starter.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 500 g chicken livers
2. 3 tbsp chicken fat (schmaltz) or vegetable oil
3. 2 large onions, diced
4. 3 hard-boiled eggs
5. 1 tsp salt
6. 1/4 tsp black pepper
7. matzo crackers, for serving

=== STEPS ===
1. Rinse livers and pat dry; remove any connective tissue or green spots.
2. Heat schmaltz in large skillet; add onions and cook until deeply golden, about 15 minutes; remove and set aside.
3. Add livers to same skillet; cook over medium-high heat until browned outside and just pink inside, about 5 minutes per side.
4. Combine livers, onions, and eggs in food processor; pulse until coarsely chopped (not smooth); season with salt and pepper.
5. Transfer to bowl; cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours; serve cold with matzo crackers.
