=== HEADNOTE ===
Savory pastry pockets filled with seasoned mashed potatoes and onions. A beloved Jewish deli classic.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
2. 1 tsp baking powder
3. 1/2 tsp salt
4. 1/3 cup vegetable oil
5. 1 egg
6. 1/2 cup warm water
7. 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
8. 2 onions, diced
9. 3 tbsp vegetable oil
10. salt and pepper to taste

=== STEPS ===
1. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt; add oil, egg, and water; knead until smooth; cover and rest 30 minutes.
2. Boil potatoes until tender; drain and mash until smooth.
3. Saute onions in oil until golden; mix with mashed potatoes; season with salt and pepper.
4. Divide dough into 12 pieces; roll each into thin circle; place 2 tbsp filling in center; gather edges and pinch to seal; flatten slightly.
5. Place seam-side down on greased baking sheet; bake at 190°C/375°F for 25–30 minutes until golden brown.
