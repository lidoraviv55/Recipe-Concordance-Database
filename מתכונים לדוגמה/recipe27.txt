=== HEADNOTE ===
Crispy twice-baked almond cookies, the Jewish cousin of Italian biscotti. Perfect for dipping in tea or coffee.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 3 eggs
2. 1 cup sugar
3. 3/4 cup vegetable oil
4. 1 tsp vanilla extract
5. 1 tsp almond extract
6. 3 cups all-purpose flour
7. 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
8. 1/4 tsp salt
9. 1 cup slivered almonds

=== STEPS ===
1. Beat eggs and sugar until light; add oil, vanilla, and almond extract; mix well.
2. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt; add to wet ingredients; fold in almonds.
3. Divide dough into 3 portions; shape each into log about 30 cm (12 inches) long; place on greased baking sheet; flatten slightly.
4. Bake at 180°C/350°F for 25 minutes until light golden; remove and let cool 10 minutes.
5. Slice logs diagonally into 1 cm (1/2 inch) pieces; lay flat on baking sheet; bake 10 minutes per side until golden and crisp.
