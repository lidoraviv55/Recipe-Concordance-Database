=== HEADNOTE ===
Light and fluffy matzo balls in rich chicken broth. The secret to fluffy matzo balls is chilling the mixture and not over-handling.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 4 eggs
2. 1/4 cup chicken fat (schmaltz) or vegetable oil
3. 1 cup matzo meal
4. 1 tsp salt
5. 1/4 tsp white pepper
6. 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped (optional)
7. 1/4 cup seltzer water or chicken broth
8. 8 cups chicken soup, homemade or prepared

=== STEPS ===
1. Beat eggs with schmaltz until blended; stir in matzo meal, salt, pepper, and dill if using; add seltzer and mix gently.
2. Cover and refrigerate at least 30 minutes or up to overnight.
3. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil; with wet hands, form mixture into balls about 3 cm (1 inch) diameter.
4. Drop balls into boiling water; reduce heat to simmer, cover and cook 30–40 minutes without lifting lid.
5. Heat chicken soup in separate pot; transfer matzo balls to soup bowls, ladle hot soup over them, and garnish with fresh dill.
