=== HEADNOTE ===
Traditional chopped herring with apple and onion—savory, slightly sweet, and spreadable.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 2 pickled herring fillets, soaked and rinsed
2. 1 medium onion, finely chopped
3. 1 tart apple, peeled and finely chopped
4. 1 slice white bread, soaked in 2 tbsp vinegar and squeezed
5. 1 tbsp neutral oil
6. 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
7. black pepper, to taste
8. 1 hard-boiled egg, chopped (for garnish)

=== STEPS ===
1. Bone the herring and chop finely.
2. Add onion, apple, soaked bread, oil, cinnamon (if using), and pepper; mix to a coarse paste.
3. Mound on a platter and garnish with chopped egg. Serve with rye bread or crackers.
