=== HEADNOTE ===
Light potato-starch sponge cake suitable for Passover.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 8 eggs, separated
2. 1 cup sugar
3. 1 cup potato starch
4. 1 tsp lemon zest
5. 1 tbsp lemon juice
6. pinch salt

=== STEPS ===
1. Heat oven to 175°C/350°F. Line an ungreased tube pan bottom with parchment.
2. Beat yolks with sugar until pale and thick; fold in potato starch, zest, and lemon juice.
3. Whip whites with salt to soft peaks; fold into batter in thirds.
4. Bake 35–40 minutes until springy. Invert to cool completely before unmolding.
