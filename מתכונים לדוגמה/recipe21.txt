=== HEADNOTE ===
Classic Eastern European comfort food combining toasted buckwheat groats with bow-tie pasta and caramelized onions.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 cup kasha (roasted buckwheat groats)
2. 1 egg, beaten
3. 2 cups boiling water or chicken broth
4. 2 cups bow-tie pasta (farfalle)
5. 3 tbsp vegetable oil
6. 2 large onions, diced
7. 1 tsp salt
8. 1/4 tsp black pepper

=== STEPS ===
1. Mix kasha with beaten egg in dry skillet; stir over medium heat until grains separate and are dry, about 3 minutes.
2. Add boiling water and salt; cover and simmer 15 minutes until liquid is absorbed and kasha is tender.
3. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions; drain and set aside.
4. Heat oil in large skillet; add onions and cook over medium-low heat 20 minutes until deeply golden and caramelized.
5. Combine kasha, pasta, and onions; season with pepper and additional salt to taste; serve warm.
