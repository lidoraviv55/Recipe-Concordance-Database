=== HEADNOTE ===
Triangular filled cookies shaped like Haman's hat, traditional for the Purim holiday. Poppy seed (mohn) filling is classic, but prune and apricot are equally beloved.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 2/3 cup sugar
2. 1/2 cup vegetable oil
3. 2 eggs
4. 1 tsp vanilla extract
5. zest of 1 orange
6. 3 cups all-purpose flour
7. 2 tsp baking powder
8. 1/4 tsp salt
9. 2 tbsp orange juice
10. 1 cup poppy seed filling (mohn), or prune/apricot jam

=== STEPS ===
1. Beat sugar and oil until combined; add eggs one at a time, then vanilla and orange zest.
2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt; add to wet ingredients alternately with orange juice; mix until smooth dough forms; wrap and refrigerate 1 hour.
3. Roll dough to 5 mm (1/4 inch) thickness on floured surface; cut 8 cm (3 inch) circles with cookie cutter or glass.
4. Place 1 tsp filling in center of each circle; fold three sides toward center to form triangle, pinching corners tightly to seal.
5. Place on parchment-lined baking sheets; bake at 180°C/350°F for 12–15 minutes until lightly golden at edges; cool completely.
