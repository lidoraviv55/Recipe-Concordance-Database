=== HEADNOTE ===
Crispy potato pancakes traditional for Hanukkah. Squeeze out as much liquid as possible for the crispiest results.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 kg (2 lb) russet potatoes, peeled
2. 1 medium onion
3. 2 eggs, beaten
4. 3 tbsp matzo meal or all-purpose flour
5. 1 tsp salt
6. 1/4 tsp black pepper
7. vegetable oil for frying
8. sour cream, for serving
9. applesauce, for serving

=== STEPS ===
1. Grate potatoes and onion on large holes of box grater or in food processor; transfer to clean kitchen towel and squeeze out as much liquid as possible.
2. Place potato mixture in large bowl; stir in eggs, matzo meal, salt, and pepper.
3. Heat 1 cm (1/2 inch) oil in large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering.
4. Drop heaping tablespoons of mixture into oil; flatten slightly with spatula; fry 3–4 minutes per side until golden and crisp; drain on paper towels.
5. Serve immediately with sour cream and applesauce; keep warm in low oven if making large batch.
