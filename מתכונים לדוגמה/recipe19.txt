=== HEADNOTE ===
Crispy breaded chicken cutlets, pounded thin for quick cooking. A weeknight favorite that children and adults adore.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
2. 1 cup all-purpose flour
3. 2 eggs, beaten
4. 1 cup breadcrumbs or matzo meal
5. 1 tsp paprika
6. 1 tsp garlic powder
7. 1 tsp salt
8. 1/2 tsp black pepper
9. vegetable oil for frying
10. lemon wedges, for serving

=== STEPS ===
1. Place chicken between plastic wrap; pound to 1 cm (1/2 inch) thickness with meat mallet.
2. Set up breading station: flour in one dish, beaten eggs in second, breadcrumbs mixed with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in third.
3. Dredge each cutlet in flour, shaking off excess; dip in egg, then coat thoroughly with seasoned breadcrumbs.
4. Heat 1 cm (1/2 inch) oil in large skillet over medium-high heat; fry cutlets 3–4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through (internal temperature 74°C/165°F).
5. Drain on paper towels; serve immediately with lemon wedges.
