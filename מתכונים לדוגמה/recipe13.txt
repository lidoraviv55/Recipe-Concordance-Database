=== HEADNOTE ===
Slow-braised brisket with onions and root vegetables. Best made a day ahead; the flavors deepen and the meat slices beautifully when cold.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 2 kg (4–5 lb) beef brisket, first cut
2. 2 tsp salt
3. 1 tsp black pepper
4. 2 tbsp vegetable oil
5. 4 large onions, sliced
6. 6 cloves garlic, minced
7. 3 carrots, cut into chunks
8. 2 cups beef broth
9. 1 cup dry red wine
10. 2 tbsp tomato paste
11. 2 bay leaves
12. 4 sprigs fresh thyme

=== STEPS ===
1. Season brisket generously with salt and pepper; heat oil in large Dutch oven over high heat; sear meat on both sides until well browned, about 5 minutes per side; remove and set aside.
2. Reduce heat to medium; add onions and cook 15 minutes until soft and golden; add garlic and cook 1 minute more.
3. Return brisket to pot, fat side up; add carrots, broth, wine, tomato paste, bay leaves, and thyme; bring to simmer.
4. Cover tightly and braise in 160°C/325°F oven for 3–4 hours, until fork-tender; let cool in liquid; refrigerate overnight.
5. Remove solidified fat from surface; slice meat against the grain; reheat gently in the braising liquid; adjust seasoning and serve with vegetables and sauce.
