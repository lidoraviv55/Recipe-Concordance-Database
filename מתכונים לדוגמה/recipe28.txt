=== HEADNOTE ===
Sweet apple and nut mixture symbolizing the mortar used by Jewish slaves in Egypt. Essential for the Passover Seder plate.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 3 medium apples, peeled and cored
2. 1 cup walnuts
3. 1/2 cup sweet red wine
4. 2 tbsp honey
5. 1 tsp ground cinnamon
6. 1/4 tsp ground ginger

=== STEPS ===
1. Chop apples finely by hand or pulse briefly in food processor; do not puree.
2. Chop walnuts coarsely.
3. Combine apples, walnuts, wine, honey, cinnamon, and ginger in bowl; mix well.
4. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour for flavors to meld.
5. Stir before serving; adjust sweetness with additional honey if desired; serve with matzo.
