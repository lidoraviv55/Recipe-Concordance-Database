=== HEADNOTE ===
Tangy pickled herring in vinegar brine with onions and spices. A traditional appetizer served on Shabbat mornings.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 4 salt herring fillets
2. 1 cup white vinegar
3. 1/2 cup water
4. 1/3 cup sugar
5. 2 onions, sliced into rings
6. 1 tbsp pickling spices
7. 2 bay leaves
8. 10 whole peppercorns

=== STEPS ===
1. Soak herring in cold water for 24 hours, changing water several times to remove excess salt; drain and pat dry.
2. Cut herring into 2 cm (1 inch) pieces.
3. Combine vinegar, water, and sugar in saucepan; heat until sugar dissolves; let cool completely.
4. Layer herring and onion rings in glass jar; add pickling spices, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
5. Pour cooled brine over fish; cover and refrigerate at least 3 days before serving; keeps refrigerated for 2 weeks.
