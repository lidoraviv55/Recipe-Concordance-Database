=== HEADNOTE ===
Sweet braised carrots with honey and dried fruit. The Yiddish word "tzimmes" means a fuss or big deal—this dish is worth the effort.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 kg carrots, peeled and sliced into coins
2. 1 cup pitted prunes
3. 1/2 cup dried apricots
4. 1/3 cup honey
5. 2 tbsp brown sugar
6. 1/4 cup orange juice
7. 2 tbsp margarine or butter
8. 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
9. 1/4 tsp ground ginger
10. 1/2 tsp salt
11. 1 cup water

=== STEPS ===
1. Place carrots, prunes, and apricots in large pot or Dutch oven.
2. Combine honey, brown sugar, orange juice, margarine, cinnamon, ginger, salt, and water in saucepan; heat until margarine melts and sugar dissolves.
3. Pour honey mixture over carrots and fruit; bring to simmer over medium heat.
4. Cover and transfer to 160°C/325°F oven; bake 1½–2 hours, stirring occasionally, until carrots are very tender and liquid is syrupy.
5. Uncover for final 20 minutes to glaze; adjust sweetness with more honey if desired; serve warm or at room temperature.
