SERVINGS: 4–6

=== HEADNOTE ===
Crisp potato cakes seasoned with onion; ideal fresh from the pan.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 1 kg potatoes, peeled and grated
2. 1 small onion, grated
3. 2 eggs, beaten
4. 1/3 cup flour (or matzo meal)
5. 1 1/4 tsp salt, or to taste
6. black pepper, to taste
7. oil for shallow frying

=== STEPS ===
1. Squeeze excess liquid from grated potatoes; combine with onion, eggs, flour, salt, and pepper.
2. Heat 0.5 cm oil in a skillet. Drop batter by spoonfuls; flatten slightly.
3. Fry until golden on both sides. Drain on paper and serve hot.
