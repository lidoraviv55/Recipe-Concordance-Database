=== HEADNOTE ===
Fresh egg noodles made from scratch. Use in soups, kugels, or tossed with butter and poppy seeds.

=== INGREDIENTS ===
1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
2. 3 eggs
3. 1/2 tsp salt
4. 1 tbsp water (if needed)

=== STEPS ===
1. Mound flour on clean surface; make well in center; add eggs and salt.
2. Beat eggs with fork, gradually incorporating flour from sides until rough dough forms; add water if too dry.
3. Knead 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic; cover and rest 30 minutes.
4. Divide dough in half; roll each piece paper-thin; let dry 15 minutes; roll up loosely and slice into strips.
5. Shake noodles loose; cook in boiling salted water 3–4 minutes until tender; drain and serve immediately.
